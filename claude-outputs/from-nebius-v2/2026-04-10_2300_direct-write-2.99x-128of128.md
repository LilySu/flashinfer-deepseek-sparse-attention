## Current Per-Batch Launch Count: ~7

1. `mod.gather_dequant_fp8(...)` — fused gather + dequant (TVM)
2. `q_b @ K.T` — cuBLAS GEMM
3. `scores.clamp_(min=0)` — in-place ReLU
4. `scores.mul_(w[:, None])` — in-place weight multiply
5. `.sum(dim=0)` — reduce
6. `torch.topk(...)` — 2 kernels (gather + radix sort)
7. `mod.fused_index_remap(...)` — writes directly to output

At B=31: ~7 × 31 = ~217 launches (down from ~434 at baseline).

## What's Left (Diminishing Returns)

The remaining 7 launches per batch are all necessary:
- GEMM, TopK (3 kernels), sum — can't touch (correctness constraints)
- gather_dequant, clamp_, mul_ — already fused/in-place

Stream pipelining is the only remaining avenue but TVM FFI doesn't respect PyTorch's stream context.
